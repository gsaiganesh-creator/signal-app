What Exactly Is An EGR?
"Think of EGR as a digital title deed for gold. You own real, physical 24K gold — you just don't have to store it."
📜
Dematerialised Security
EGR is a SEBI-notified security (Dec 2021 notification). Held in your existing demat account — same as your NSE stocks. No new account needed.
🏛️
Backed by Physical Gold
Every EGR is 100% backed by real gold stored in SEBI-accredited vaults. Vault managers: Sequel Logistics, Malca-Amit JK Logistics, Brinks India.
🔁
Convert Anytime
Want the physical gold? Submit a redemption request (10AM–3PM window, 3-day validity). Physical gold withdrawn from vault and delivered.
🌍
One Nation, One Price
NSE sets a single transparent price across India. No more paying ₹200/gram premium at your local jeweller based on their margin.
How It Works — Step by Step
1
Gold Deposited in Vault
Refiners/jewellers deposit physical gold (meeting LBMA/BIS standards) with an accredited Vault Manager. First 1,000g bar deposited on launch day itself.
2
EGR Created in Demat
Vault converts gold to electronic receipt. EGR is credited to seller's demat account — dematerialised and listed on NSE EGR segment.
3
You Buy on NSE
You place a buy order through your broker (Zerodha, Groww, Angel etc.) for 1g, 10g, 100g, or 1kg denomination. Traded like stocks in real-time.
4
EGR Credited to Your Demat
T+1 settlement. EGR appears in your demat account the next working day. NSE Clearing Corporation guarantees settlement.
5
Sell or Redeem Anytime
Sell EGR on exchange for cash (T+1), OR submit redemption request to get the actual gold bar delivered to you.

The 7 Advantages Explained
🇮🇳
1. One Nation, One Price
Every Indian — from Mumbai to Manipur — pays the exact same market price. No dealer margin, no city premium, no Friday afternoon "rate ke hisaab se" nonsense. Price discovered on NSE's matching engine.
💎
2. Certified 999/995 Purity — Guaranteed
Gold meets LBMA (London Bullion Market Association) and BIS standards. Zero doubt on hallmarking. Physical gold purity at jewellers can vary — EGR cannot. You're always getting exactly what you paid for.
⚡
3. T+1 Liquidity — Sell in Seconds
Try selling a 100g gold bar quickly at fair price. You'll lose 3–5% just getting it appraised. EGR: place sell order, money in account next working day. Exchange-guaranteed settlement.
📦
4. Zero Storage Headache
No bank locker (₹5,000–₹15,000/yr charges + limited hours). No home storage risk. No insurance premium. Gold sits in institutional vault — guarded 24/7. Your demat account is your locker.
—
1️⃣
5. Start with Just 1 Gram
At ₹7,500–8,000 per gram, you can start investing immediately. No need to save up for 10g coin (₹80,000+). Perfect for SIP-style gold accumulation each month. Democratises gold investment.
🔁
6. Physical Redemption Available
Unlike Gold ETFs (fund units, not physical gold), EGR can be surrendered for actual gold delivery. Want the bar for your daughter's wedding? Convert EGR → physical gold. The best of both worlds.
🏛️
7. SEBI Regulated + Settlement Guaranteed
Digital Gold platforms (Paytm, PhonePe gold) had SEBI concerns in Nov 2025. EGR is a notified security under the Securities Contracts Act. NSE Clearing Corporation guarantees every trade. No platform risk.
⚖️
EGR vs Sovereign Gold Bond — The Nuance
SGB pays 2.5% interest + tax-free maturity gains (if held 8 yrs). EGR has better liquidity, no lock-in, physical redemption. SGB wins on returns. EGR wins on flexibility. For traders: EGR. For long hold: SGB.
