Executive Summary
The gap in the Indian retail trading market
The Opportunity

India has 90 million+ demat accounts, yet every trading app (Zerodha, Groww, mStock, Angel One) is a glorified order-entry system. None explain why to buy or sell. None generate deployable code. None integrate social sentiment. SIGNAL is the first Indian app to bridge ML intelligence with the retail trader.

The Core Thesis

A trader who understands why a signal is generated will trust it, learn from it, and retain subscription. Explainability is the product moat — not just the signals themselves.

Risks to Address
Honest assessment of challenges
SEBI Registration Required
Providing investment signals may require SEBI Research Analyst registration (RA). Algo Builder is educational — but must be framed carefully to avoid "investment advice" classification.
Broker API Access Delays
Zerodha Kite API, mStock API approval takes 1–3 business days. For launch, Excel import must be robust fallback. Rate limits (1 req/sec) require smart caching.
𝕏 API Cost at Scale
𝕏 API Basic tier limits 1M tweets/month. At scale, Pro tier (~$5K/mo) required. Include Twitter sentiment only from Starter plan onwards to control costs.
ML Model Accuracy
Indian markets have unique behaviour (SGX Nifty gap-ups, budget day volatility, T+1 settlement impact). Model must be trained on NSE-specific data, not US benchmarks.
